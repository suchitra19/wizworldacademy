Welcome to Wizworld Academy

Welcome to Wizworld Academy, where the adventure of learning begins! Our mission is to provide a nurturing and stimulating environment that fosters the growth and development of young minds. At Wizworld Academy, we believe that every child is unique and deserves an education that respects their individuality and encourages their natural curiosity.

Kindergarten is a vital stage in a child's educational journey, emphasizing a balanced development of academic skills, social-emotional growth, physical coordination, and creative expression. By focusing on these key areas, kindergarten programs prepare children for the structured learning environment of elementary school while fostering a love for learning and a sense of curiosity about the world around them.

School Facilities

Classroom

In a well organize classroom space, children are inspired to interact with one another and with the environment in ways that promote a higher level of meaningful learning and creativity.

Activity Room

They provide structured yet flexible environments that cater to various learning styles and developmental needs, promoting active, engaging, and meaningful learning experiences.

Play Area

Opportunities for physical activity, cognitive challenges, social interaction, and emotional expression, play areas help children develop essential life skills in an enjoyable and engaging way.

Gym Area

By providing a safe and stimulating environment for physical activity, the gym helps children develop essential life skills and fosters a lifelong appreciation for fitness and healthy living.

Dine Area

By providing a structured, hygienic, and socially engaging environment, the dining area helps instill healthy habits, fosters a sense of community, and teaches important life skills.It playing a crucial role.

AV Room

With multisensory learning opportunities,coupled with appropriate technology and content, this specialized room can inspire creativity,critical thinking,and lifelong love for learning.

Library

A diverse and engaging collection of books and resources helps children develop a love for reading and learning that can last a lifetime. It fosters early literacy, cognitive growth.

CCTV surveillance

CCTV surveillance in a kindergarten can enhance safety, security, and peace of mind for parents and staff. Kindergarten can effectively use CCTV to create a safer environment for children.
